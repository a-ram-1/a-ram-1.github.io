Hi, I'm A. I'm a PhD student in Yale's Computational Biology and Bioinformatics program working with Rick Altice and Forrest Crawford among others. My work looks at Connecticut's warning system for opioid overdose spikes and aims to 1) identify the causal effect of this system on overdose rates 2) understand barriers to overdose prevention, utilization of the spike alert system and preferences for alternatives 3) create an alternative approach able to address some of the shortcomings of the status quo. In the past I've also worked on projects related to trans healthcare.

Before coming to Yale, I did my BS and MEng in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at MIT. You can reach me at a[dot]ram[at]yale[dot]edu, especially if you want help accessing paywalled journal articles or other online resources I have access to through Yale.
